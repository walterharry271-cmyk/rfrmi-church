Our Story

A Church Born for Revival

We are a community of believers in Ikere-Ekiti, Ekiti State, called together by God's grace and set apart for a purpose — to see revival fire spread across our land and beyond.

Our youth department stands at the forefront of this mission: young hearts ablaze with faith, shaped by the Word, and sent into the world as messengers of the Gospel.

"But you are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, His own special people, that you may proclaim the praises of Him who called you out of darkness into His marvellous light." — 1 Peter 2:9
